Lava Cake Ingredients

  • Butter: A stick of butter lends richness, moisture, and flavor.

How to Make Chocolate Lava Cake

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make the best lava cake of your life:

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together using a double boiler.

  2. Beat the eggs and sugar together, then add the chocolate and remaining ingredients.

  3. Bake until the sides are set but the center is jiggly.
